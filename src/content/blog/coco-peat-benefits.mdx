Coco peat is revolutionizing agriculture with its exceptional properties. Learn how this sustainable growing medium can transform your farming practices.

## What is Coco Peat?

Coco peat is a natural fiber made from coconut husks, offering excellent properties for plant growth:

- High water retention
- Excellent aeration
- pH balanced
- Pathogen-free

## Benefits for African Agriculture

1. **Water Management**
   - Reduces irrigation frequency
   - Prevents water logging
   - Maintains moisture levels

2. **Root Development**
   - Promotes healthy growth
   - Prevents root diseases
   - Improves nutrient uptake
