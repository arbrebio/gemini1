Precision Agriculture in Africa

Precision agriculture transforms African farms with modern technology — greenhouses, drip irrigation, coco peat substrates and foliar fertilizers.

Greenhouses & Tunnels

Our tunnel and multi-span greenhouses enable year-round production in Africa regardless of rainy seasons.

Drip & Sprinkler Irrigation

Drip irrigation saves up to 70% water and enables direct root-zone fertigation.

Coco Peat & Coconut Fibre Substrates

Coco peat is the ideal substrate for Africa: excellent water retention, root aeration, 100% natural.

Fertilizers & Crop Protection

Precise fertilization with NPK, foliar sprays and approved crop protection products maximizes yield safely.

Agronomic Monitoring & Technical Advice

Beyond equipment sales, Arbre Bio Africa supports each farmer with personalised agronomic follow-up.

FAQ: Precision Agriculture in Africa

What is precision agriculture and why is it suited for Africa?

Precision agriculture uses modern technologies to maximise yields while reducing inputs — ideal for Africa's variable climate.

What is the cost of a greenhouse in Côte d'Ivoire?

Greenhouse costs in Côte d'Ivoire vary by type and size. Contact us for a personalised quote.

How does drip irrigation improve yields in Africa?

Drip irrigation improves yields through precise water delivery, fertigation, and reduced foliage disease.

What is the difference between coco peat, coir fibre and coco peat substrate?

Coco fibre is the coarse fibrous part; coco peat is the fine spongy part — both improve tropical soil structure.

Does Arbre Bio Africa deliver across West Africa?

Yes, we deliver equipment and inputs across West Africa with installation, training and on-site support.
