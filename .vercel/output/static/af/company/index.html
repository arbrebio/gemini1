Our Mission: Revolutionise African Agriculture Through Precision

Arbre Bio Africa was born from a simple observation: African farmers have access to the same crops and markets as their European counterparts — but not the same tools.

Why Arbre Bio Africa Exists

Africa feeds 1.4 billion people with less than 5% of global agricultural investment. African farmers often lack controlled water, improved seeds, professional substrates or adapted fertilisers — resulting in yields 3-10× lower than those achieved with the same crops in developed countries.

Arbre Bio Africa provides solutions to reverse this trend: climate-adapted greenhouses, precision drip irrigation, coco peat substrates, fertilisers and crop protection — with complete agronomic follow-up. Our goal: every farmer in Côte d'Ivoire and West Africa can double their yields and access premium markets.

FAQ: About Arbre Bio Africa

What is Arbre Bio Africa?

Arbre Bio Africa is a precision agriculture solutions company for Africa. Founded by Sydney, a precision agriculture expert, it provides greenhouses, drip irrigation, coco peat substrates, fertilisers and crop protection products adapted to African conditions. Based in Côte d'Ivoire, it operates across West Africa.

What are Arbre Bio Africa's areas of expertise?

Arbre Bio Africa specialises in: 1) Agricultural greenhouses (tunnel, multi-span, high-tech). 2) Precision irrigation (drip, sprinkler, fertigation). 3) Horticultural substrates (coco peat, coir, perlite, vermiculite). 4) Fertilisation and crop protection. 5) Agronomic monitoring and technical advice. 6) Training for African agricultural teams.

In which countries does Arbre Bio Africa operate?

Arbre Bio Africa operates primarily from Côte d'Ivoire and delivers solutions across West and Central Africa: Senegal, Ghana, Burkina Faso, Mali, Togo, Benin, Cameroon, Gabon. We facilitate import-export and logistics for all ECOWAS countries.

How to contact Arbre Bio Africa?

Contact Arbre Bio Africa by WhatsApp at +225 05 00 55 25 25 (fast response 7 days/week), by email at farms@arbrebio.com, or via our website contact form. For a personalised quote on greenhouses, irrigation or substrates, we typically respond within 24 business hours.

Does Arbre Bio Africa offer financing for agricultural equipment?

Arbre Bio Africa can support farmers and agri-entrepreneurs in accessing agricultural financing in West Africa: agricultural investment funds, microfinancing, agricultural cooperatives and development aid programmes. We prepare the technical files needed for financing applications.
