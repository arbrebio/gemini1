Why Precision Irrigation is Essential in Africa

West Africa faces growing water challenges: prolonged droughts, irregular rainfall. Precision irrigation is no longer a luxury but an economic necessity.

Conventional systems waste 40–60% of water. Precision drip delivers water directly to the root zone at over 90% efficiency.

Arbre Bio Africa designs and installs custom irrigation for all farm sizes, from small market gardens to large commercial greenhouses.

Maximum Water Efficiency
Up to 90% efficiency vs 50–60% for traditional sprinklers.
Accelerated Crop Growth
Constant root-zone moisture promotes steady growth and reduces water stress.
Integrated Fertigation
Deliver fertilisers directly through your irrigation network, cutting costs 30%.
Automated & Connected Control
Smart controllers with scheduling, moisture sensors and remote smartphone management.

Irrigation FAQ

Which irrigation system suits market garden crops in West Africa?

For market garden crops, drip irrigation offers the best water efficiency and fertigation compatibility in West Africa.

What does drip irrigation installation cost in Côte d'Ivoire?

Installation cost depends on area, crop type and equipment. Contact us for a free study and detailed quote.

Can drip irrigation be coupled with a fertigation system?

Yes. Fertigation reduces fertiliser losses by 30–50% and improves nutrient uptake. All our drip systems are fertigation-compatible.

Does Arbre Bio Africa handle maintenance of installed irrigation systems?

Yes. We offer preventive and corrective maintenance contracts with regular inspections, filter cleaning and rapid on-site intervention.

How much water can drip irrigation save versus traditional methods?

A well-designed drip system saves 40–70% of water versus surface irrigation, exceeding 70% with smart soil moisture sensors.
