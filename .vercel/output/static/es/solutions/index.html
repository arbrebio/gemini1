FAQ: Soluciones de Agricultura de Precisión en África

What are the best agricultural solutions to start with in Africa?

We recommend starting with: 1) A simple tunnel greenhouse (100-500m²). 2) Drip irrigation for water saving and fertigation. 3) Coco peat as soilless substrate. This combination has the best investment/yield ratio with average 14-month ROI.

What budget is needed for a greenhouse vegetable farm in Africa?

Budget varies by scale: a pilot unit (500m²) requires 5-15 million FCFA (8,000-25,000 €) including equipment. Commercial scale (1 ha) needs 50-150 million FCFA depending on technology level. We offer scalable solutions and can facilitate agricultural financing.

What is the difference between drip irrigation and sprinkler irrigation?

Drip irrigation delivers water directly to roots, saves 70% water, enables fertigation and reduces foliar disease. Sprinklers simulate rain and suit large fields and broad crops (cereals, coffee, cocoa). We offer both depending on farm profile.

Does Arbre Bio Africa offer precision agriculture training?

Yes, all our solutions include comprehensive training: greenhouse installation and maintenance, irrigation programming, fertilisation and substrate management, phytosanitary diagnosis and IPM. We also offer standalone training for agri-entrepreneurs.

How does Arbre Bio Africa guarantee the stated yields?

Our yield guarantees are based on: 1) Field data from our clients since 2020. 2) Validated varietal trials and agronomic protocols. 3) Monthly agronomist monitoring. The +200-300% yields are achievable with the complete protocol (greenhouse + irrigation + substrate + fertilisation + IPM).
